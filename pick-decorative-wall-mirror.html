Why this mirror works

A decorative wall mirror is one of the most transformative pieces you can add to any room. It reflects natural and artificial light, making spaces feel brighter and more open. The right mirror serves as both a functional item and a stunning focal point that ties your decor together.

  • A mirror like this helps bounce both daylight and lamplight so the room feels brighter and less flat.
  • The frame gives the wall a more finished focal point without adding another bulky piece of furniture.
  • It works above consoles, mantels, dressers, and entry tables where you want function and visual lift at the same time.
