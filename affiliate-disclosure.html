Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Dreamy Decor contains affiliate links on some pages. If you click one of those links and make a purchase, Dreamy Decor may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

What this means
Affiliate links help support the site, but they do not change the retail price you pay on the merchant's website.

How affiliate links appear

Affiliate links may appear in decor pick pages, product roundups, blog posts, or other pages that recommend products or retailers.

Editorial independence

Dreamy Decor aims to recommend products based on usefulness, fit, and design relevance. A product being affiliated does not guarantee it is right for every room, budget, or use case.

Retailer control

Merchants control product pricing, availability, shipping, return rules, and final purchase terms. Those details can change at any time after a page is published.

Your responsibility as a buyer

  • Check dimensions, finishes, stock status, and total cost on the retailer's site before purchasing.
  • Review the retailer's return policy and delivery terms before placing an order.
  • Use your own judgment when comparing products and deciding whether a recommendation fits your space.
