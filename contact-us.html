Contact Us

For general questions, content corrections, affiliate disclosure questions, or privacy-related requests, email hello@dreamydecor.ai.

Best use
Email is the best option for link corrections, retailer issues, disclosure questions, or requests related to information you have sent directly to the site.

When you contact Dreamy Decor

  • Include the page URL if your message is about a specific article or product pick.
  • Keep screenshots or order details limited to what is actually needed for context.
  • Do not send payment card numbers, government IDs, or other highly sensitive information by email.

Retailer questions

If your question is about shipping, returns, refunds, stock status, or order support after you leave Dreamy Decor, contact the retailer directly because those transactions are handled on the merchant's site.
