Small space decor hacks: making a tiny apartment feel huge

Living in a small space doesn't mean you have to sacrifice style. With the right small space decor hacks, you can create a home that feels airy, organized, and intentional.

Tip
Think vertically. When floor space is limited, the walls are your best friend.

1. Use Leggy Furniture

Furniture that sits directly on the floor can feel heavy and block visual flow. Opt for sofas, chairs, and consoles with tapered legs. Seeing the floor continue under your furniture tricks the eye into thinking the room is larger.

2. The Mirror Trick

It's the oldest trick in the book because it works. A large floor mirror or a gallery of smaller mirrors reflects light and views, instantly doubling the visual depth of a room.

3. Smart Apartment Storage

Clutter is the enemy of small spaces. Use multi-functional furniture like storage ottomans, bed frames with drawers, or a coffee table that lifts to reveal hidden compartments.

4. Keep the Palette Light

While dark colors are cozy, light neutrals (whites, creams, light grays) reflect more light and make walls feel like they are receding. Save the bold colors for accents like pillows or art.
