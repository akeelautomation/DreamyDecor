Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Summary
Dreamy Decor keeps personal data collection light. This site may receive basic analytics and server log data, and affiliate partners may use their own tracking when you click outbound links.

Information we collect

Dreamy Decor may collect or receive the following categories of information:

  • Basic server data such as IP address, browser type, referring page, timestamps, and requested URLs.
  • Aggregate site analytics used to understand which pages are visited and how visitors use the site.
  • Information you voluntarily send if you contact the site by email.
  • Affiliate attribution data created when you click a merchant link.

How we use information

Information may be used to operate the site, monitor performance, respond to messages, improve content, and report affiliate referrals.

Cookies and similar technologies

Dreamy Decor itself aims to keep tracking minimal, but third-party services such as hosting, analytics providers, merchants, and affiliate networks may use cookies, pixels, or similar technologies for measurement and attribution.

Affiliate links and third-party sites

Some pages link to third-party retailers. If you leave Dreamy Decor and visit a merchant site, that third party controls its own privacy practices, cookies, pricing, availability, and account policies.

Data retention

Server logs, analytics records, and email correspondence may be retained only as long as reasonably needed for security, operations, reporting, legal compliance, or support purposes.

Your choices

  • You can limit cookies through your browser settings.
  • You can avoid affiliate tracking by not clicking outbound retailer links.
  • You can contact Dreamy Decor to request review or deletion of messages you have sent directly.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email hello@dreamydecor.ai.
